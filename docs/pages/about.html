Personal Statement

Hello! I’m a Marketing Analytics student at Cal Poly Pomona with a genuine passion for data and the stories it can tell. My academic background spans quantitative research methods, consumer behavior, and digital marketing strategy — and I’ve spent the past two years applying those frameworks to real datasets.

I believe the most valuable analysts are those who can move fluidly between the technical and the strategic, equally comfortable in a boardroom presenting insights to a non-technical audience.
